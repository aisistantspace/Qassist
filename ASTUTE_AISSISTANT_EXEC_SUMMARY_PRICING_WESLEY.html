1) Executive Summary

Astute AIssistant is a multilingual AI customer conversation platform that helps organizations respond instantly, capture opportunities, and escalate urgent cases while keeping customer interactions clear and personal.

A key differentiator is support for Papiamentu, alongside Dutch, English, and Spanish. For Curacao, Aruba, Bonaire, and surrounding islands, Papiamentu is essential for trust, clarity, and customer adoption.

For standard managed deployments, Astute AIssistant is hosted on infrastructure operated under European GDPR legal frameworks, with privacy and personal-data handling aligned to GDPR requirements.

  • 24/7 AI customer conversations
  • Knowledge-based answering (business-specific responses)
  • Lead capture, qualification, and guided forms
  • Smart routing to the right department (sales, support, claims, admin)
  • Escalation to human teams for complex or urgent support
  • Ticket and follow-up workflow support
  • Dashboard visibility for operations and management

B) Dedicated for Insurers (Single-Tenant) - For insurance organizations requiring dedicated setup, stronger control, and tailored workflows.

5) Custom Hosting & AI Model Option

If a customer requests a non-standard deployment, this is handled as custom work and re-estimated per project.

  • Self-hosted deployment on customer-owned infrastructure
  • Dedicated VPS or private cloud environment
  • Private LLM hosting
  • Azure-hosted OpenAI model setup
  • Custom security/networking requirements outside standard packages
  • All third-party infrastructure and model costs are paid by the customer
  • Implementation, setup, integration, and support are quoted separately
  • A new scope and estimate is approved before work starts
